Meet the Dinosaur Friends

Once upon a time, in a land with big trees and pretty flowers, lived five little dinosaur friends.

There was Tilly the Triceratops (who was nervous), Rex the T-Rex (who was super excited), Steggy the Stegosaurus (who had accidents), Ptera the Pterodactyl (who was first to succeed), and Bronto the Brachiosaurus (who was scared of big potties).

They loved to play! But they were learning something new - how to use the potty. Just like you!

Tilly's Big Feelings

Tilly was playing when her tummy felt funny. It felt tight and full, like it had bubbles inside.

Her body was telling her something. 'Oh!' she thought. 'I need to go potty!'

But Tilly was scared. "What if I fall in? The potty looks so big!"

But her mommy said: 'It's okay, Tilly! Let's go together!'

Rex Tries the Potty

Rex got a new potty chair! It was bright green and just his size.

He sat down and waited. He wiggled a little. He took deep breaths. But nothing came out.

Rex felt frustrated. His face got red. He wanted to do it right.

Daddy sang: 'Potty, potty, here we go, take your time and let it flow!'

Steggy's Accidents Happen

Steggy was playing with his favorite toy blocks. He was building a tall tower.

His tummy felt a little funny, but he was too busy to listen. He wanted to finish his tower.

Then suddenly - oops! His pants felt warm and wet. He had an accident.

But his grandma said: 'Accidents happen! That's how we learn. Next time, listen to your body!'

Ptera's Potty Success

Ptera had been practicing every day. She listened to her body carefully.

One morning, she felt her tummy get tight. She knew what that meant!

She flew to her potty chair, pulled down her pants, sat down, and - success! She did it!

'Mommy, mommy! I used the potty! I listened to my body!'

Then Ptera helped her friends. "Listen to your body! It tells you when!"

Bronto Washes His Hands

After using the potty, Bronto knew he needed to wash his hands.

He stood by the sink. He turned on warm water. He got soap on his hands.

He sang: 'Wash, wash, wash your hands, make them clean and bright! Front and back and in between, now they're clean and right!'

He rinsed all the bubbles away. 'Clean hands!' said his sister.

The Potty Training Parade

One sunny morning, the friends had a parade! They wore their big kid undies.

They shared tips:

• Listen to your body!

• Accidents are okay!

• Keep trying!

• Wash your hands!

• Help your friends!

• Nighttime takes practice!

• Different potties are okay!

Celebrate every success! Hooray!

Other little dinosaurs watched and clapped. Everyone was learning together!
